Fried Eggs

Description

It tastes the best in the breakfast!

Ingridients

  • Eggs
  • Sault to taste
  • Spices to taste

Steps

  1. Wash the eggs
  2. Warm up the pan
  3. Add eggs to the hot pan, then add some sault and spices
  4. Cover with a lid and wait for about 5 minutes
  5. Done! Good apetite!
